About Us

Since our opening in 1997, we have operated as a family-owned restaurant. We serve a huge range of freshly prepared Mexican meals! NO microwaves. NO msg. NO preservatives. NO freezers. NO lard. Dine-in and take-out options are available. Come in and enjoy a family meal or business lunch.

Location: Valencia, CA

Year Established: 1997

A little bit about us:

At Wana Iguana Fresh Baja Grill, we are committed to using only the freshest ingredients in our cooking, with NO lard, NO preservatives, NO MSG, NO microwaves, and NO freezers. We believe in the importance of serving fresh, healthy food and always going the extra mile to deliver the best possible dining experience.
Our Menu: Our menu features a variety of traditional Mexican dishes, all made with love and care by our team.
Our Atmosphere: In addition to serving up delicious food, when you dine with us at Wana Iguana Fresh Baja Grill, you can expect a lively atmosphere filled with the sounds of your favorite tunes.

Community Involvement:

We try to be an active participant in local causes, such as Amigos De La Feria in Lancaster, CA, where we work to increase Hispanic representation and contribute to community work. Additionally, we are proud to contribute to many organizations in the Santa Clarita Valley such as many of our local schools. We invite you to come and enjoy a taste of Mexico at Wana Iguana Fresh Mexican Grill. Whether you're a regular or a first-time visitor, we can't wait to welcome you to our family and serve you some of the best Mexican food in Valencia, CA. Thank you for choosing us and we hope to see you soon!
